Biography

I am a Ph.D. student in the Computer Science department at the University of Texas at San Antonio starting Fall 2016. I have earned my M.Sc. in Computational Mathematics from Lomonosov Moscow State University, Russia in 2015. I am currently working on Web-search, Large-scale In-memory Storage Engines, and Data Integration.
